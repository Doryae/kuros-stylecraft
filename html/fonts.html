Some quick notes

Fonts are a very important aspect of a website. The size of the line of text is relevant too.

Just keep in mind : 2 to 3 fonts max for a clean user experience.

Between 50 to 75 characters per line of text.

Sans-serif is easier to read if the text is long.
Serif is often used for headings.
Monospace is more used for code, for exemple. They can be of any type seen above. Manuscript is not always a good idea... But can bring a bit of charm.
